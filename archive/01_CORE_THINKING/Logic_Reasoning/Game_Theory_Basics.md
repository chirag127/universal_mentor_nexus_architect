# Game Theory Basics

## SUMMARY

Game Theory is the mathematical study of strategic decision-making. It analyzes situations where the outcome for an individual (a "player") depends on the actions taken by other players. Key concepts include players, strategies, payoffs, and equilibrium. It models rational decision-makers aiming to optimize their own outcomes, even when those outcomes are interdependent. Fundamental to understanding strategic interactions are concepts like the Prisoner's Dilemma, Nash Equilibrium, and zero-sum vs. non-zero-sum games.
